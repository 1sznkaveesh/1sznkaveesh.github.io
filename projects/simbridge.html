F1 Sim Rig Telemetry Bridge

Active Deployment / Updates Ongoing

This codebase builds out the core software translation layer for a motion simulation flight and racing rig. Designed directly around the Assetto Corsa physics engine ecosystem, the framework establishes high-frequency hooks to intercept real-time game telemetry data packet matrices.

Once caught, the bridge parses live acceleration vector sheets, angular velocities, and structural vibration values, converting raw simulator outputs into direct control instructions to drive external physical motion mechanisms.

Development Roadmap
  • Integration of complete custom SMC3 hardware driver configurations.
  • Custom making our own SMC3 mixer.
